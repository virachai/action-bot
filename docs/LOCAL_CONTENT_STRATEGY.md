> **Project: "The Digital Town Crier" (Hyper-Local Automation)**

This plan outlines how to use the `auto-short-factory` to dominate a specific local area's attention economy and monetize through direct local business partnerships.

## üéØ Objective
Create a dominant local media channel (TikTok/Reels) for a specific district/province that serves as the "Go-To" source for what to eat, do, and know, generated 90% by AI.

---

## üí° The Core Concept: "Hyper-Local at Scale"
Instead of competing with global creators, we compete with... nobody. Most local news is boring or slow. We make it fast, visual, and daily.

### Content Pillars (What to make?)
1.  **Daily Digest**: "3 Things Happening in [City] Today" (Weather, Traffic, Big Events).
2.  **Hidden Gems**: "Review" a local restaurant simply by summarizing its best Google Maps reviews into a script.
3.  **Real Estate**: "House of the Week" (Scraped from property listings).
4.  **Community Voice**: Summarizing top posts from the local "Complaint" or "Community" Facebook Group (The most viral content).

---

## üí∞ Revenue Models (How to make money?)

### Model 1: The "Digital Coupon" (Sponsorship)
*   **Mechanism**: Partner with a local restaurant.
*   **Video**: "This cafe in [Area] has the best croissants..."
*   **Hook**: "Show this video for 10% off."
*   **Revenue**: Flat fee ($50-$100/video) or Commission per redemption.

### Model 2: Lead Generation (High Ticket)
*   **Target**: Real Estate Agents, Car Dealerships, Insurance Brokers.
*   **Video**: Showcase a new condo project.
*   **Call to Action**: "Click link to book a viewing."
*   **Revenue**: $10-$50 per qualified lead.

### Model 3: "Google Maps" SEO Service
*   **Target**: Businesses with low reviews.
*   **Service**: "We will make 5 short videos about your business to flood the search results."
*   **Revenue**: Monthly retainer ($300/mo).

---

## üõ†Ô∏è Execution Strategy

### Phase 1: Data Gathering (The "Local Sensors")
*   **Technical**: Build a scraper for:
    *   Specific Facebook Groups (e.g., "Bangkok Foodies").
    *   Google Maps API (Filter: "New restaurants last week").
    *   Local Events websites (Ticketmelon, Eventbrite).

### Phase 2: Cultural Calibration (The "Local Voice")
*   **AI Tweak**: Modify the Gemini prompt to use **Local Slang/Dialect**.
    *   *Prompt*: "You are a witty Bangkok local. Use words like 'Bangkokian', 'BTS Skytrain', and distinct Thai-English slang."
*   **Visuals**: Use Google Street View logic to grab real background images of the location if stock footage isn't available.

### Phase 3: Distribution
*   **Geo-Tagging**: Crucial. Every video must be tagged with the specific location in TikTok/Instagram to hit the right algorithm feed.
*   **Community Management**: Auto-reply to comments to boost engagement.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Challenges
1.  **Trust**: Local audiences sniff out "fake" content easily. The AI must sound authentic, not robotic.
2.  **Accuracy**: If the AI says "Traffic is bad" and it's clear, you lose credibility. Need real-time data verification.
3.  **Sales**: This model requires **Sales Effort** (calling local businesses), unlike the "AdSense" model which is passive.
